Photography was by Ron Burkey, and transcription was performed by a team of volunteers. Note that the page images presented online are of reduced quality, and that additional images may be available. 	GAP: ASSEMBLE REVISION 072 OF AGC PROGRAM ARTEMIS

058220,000023:                                                                                                  #  CDUTRIG, CDUTRIG1, CDUTRIG2, AND CD*GR*GS ALL COMPUTE THE SINES AND
058221,000024:                                                                                                  #  COSINES OF THREE 2'S COMPLEMENT ANGLES AND PLACE THE RESULT, DOUBLE
058222,000025:                                                                                                  #  PRECISION, IN THE SAME ORDER AS THE INPUTS, AT SINCDU AND COSCDU.  AN
058223,000026:                                                                                                  #  ADDITIONAL OUTPUT IS THE 1'S COMPLEMENT ANGLES AT CDUSPOT.  THESE
058224,000027:                                                                                                  #  ROUTINES GO OUT OF THEIR WAY TO LEAVE THE MPAC AREA AS THEY FIND IT,
058225,000028:                                                                                                  #  EXCEPT FOR THE GENERALLY UNIMPORTANT MPAC +2.  THEY DIFFER ONLY IN
058226,000029:                                                                                                  #  WHERE THEY GET THE ANGLES, AND IN METHOD OF CALLING.
058227,000030: 
058228,000031:                                                                                                  #  CDUTRIG (AND CDUTRIG1, WHICH CAN BE CALLED IN BASIC) COMPUTE THE
058229,000032:                                                                                                  #  SINES AND COSINES FROM THE CURRENT CONTENTS OF THE CDU REGISTERS.
058230,000033:                                                                                                  #  THE CONTENTS OF CDUTEMP, ETC., ARE NOT TOUCHED SO THAT THEY MAY
058231,000034:                                                                                                  #  CONTINUE TO FORM A CONSISTENT SET WITH THE LATEST PIPA READINGS.
058232,000035: 
058233,000036:                                                                                                  #  CDUTRIG1 IS LIKE CDUTRIG EXCEPT THAT IT CAN BE CALLED IN BASIC.
058234,000037: 
058235,000038:                                                                                                  #  CD*TR*GS FINDS CDU VALUES IN CDUSPOT RATHER THAN IN CDUTEMP.  THIS
058236,000039:                                                                                                  #  ALLOWS USERS TO MAKE TRANSFORMATIONS USING ARBITRARY ANGLES, OR REAL
058237,000040:                                                                                                  #  ANGLES IN AN ORDER OTHER THAN X Y Z.  A CALL TO THIS ROUTINE IS
058238,000041:                                                                                                  #  NECESSARY IN PREPARATION FOR A CALL TO AX*SR*T IN EITHER OF ITS TWO
058239,000042:                                                                                                  #  MODES (SMNB OR NBSM).  SINCE AX*SR*T EXPECTS TO FIND THE SINES AND
058240,000043:                                                                                                  #  COSINES IN THE ORDER Y Z X THE ANGLES MUST HAVE BEEN PLACED IN CDUSPOT
058241,000044:                                                                                                  #  IN THIS ORDER.  CD*TR*GS NEED NOT BE REPEATED WHEN AX*SR*T IS CALLED
058242,000045:                                                                                                  #  MORE THAN ONCE, PROVIDED THE ANGLES HAVE NOT CHANGED.  NOTE THAT SINCE
058243,000046:                                                                                                  #  IT CLOBBERS BUF2 (IN THE SINE AND COSINE ROUTINES) CD*TR*GS CANNOT BE
058244,000047:                                                                                                  #  CALLED USING BANKCALL.  SORRY.
058245,000048: 
058246,000049:                                                                                                  #  CD*TR*G IS LIKE CD*TR*GS EXCEPT THAT IT CAN BE CALLED IN
058247,000050:                                                                                                  #  INTERPRETIVE.
