041079,000103:                                                                                                 #       CONTROL ACCELERATION CONACC, OR ITS RECIPROCAL, 1/CONACC, THE TVC

041081,000105:                                                                                                 #       FIRING TIME IS SIMPLY THAT REQUIRED TO ACHIEVE THE DESIRED OGARATE,
041082,000106:                                                                                                 #       SUBJECT TO TEH LIMITATIONS DISCUSSED UNDER FUNCTIONAL DESCRIPTION,
041083,000107:                                                                                                 #       ABOVE.
041084,000108: 
041085,000109:                                                                                                 #  THE THREE CONTROL REGIONS  (+, -, AND ZERO TORQUE) ARE COMPRISED OF
041086,000110:                                                                                                 #       TWELVE SUBSET REGIONS  ( 1...6, AND THE CORRESPONDING 1-PRIME...
041087,000111:                                                                                                 #       6-PRIME )  SEE SECTION 3 OF THE GSOP  (SUNDISK OR COLOSSUS)
041088,000112: 
041089,000113:                                                                                                 # # Page 947
041090,000114:                                                                                                 #  GIVEN THE OPERATING POINT NOT IN THE COAST REGION, THE DESIRED OGARATE
041091,000115:                                                                                                 #       IS AT THE POINT OF PENETRATION OF THE COAST REGION BY THE CONTROL
041092,000116:                                                                                                 #       PARABOLA WHICH PASSES THROUGH THE OPERATING POINT. FOR REGION 3
041093,000117:                                                                                                 #       DESIRED OGARATE IS SIMPLY +-MAXLIM. FOR REGIONS 1 OR 6 THE SOLUTION
041094,000118:                                                                                                 #       TO A QUADRATIC IS REQUIRED (THE PENETRATION IS ALONG THE STRAIGHT
041095,000119:                                                                                                 #       LINE OR MINLIM BOUNDRY SWITCH LINES). AN APPROXIMATION IS MADE
041096,000120:                                                                                                 #       INSTEAD. CONSIDER AN OPERATING POINT IN REGION 6. PASS A TANGENT TO
041097,000121:                                                                                                 #       THE CONTROL PARABOLA THROUGH THE OPERATING POINT, AND FIND ITS
041098,000122:                                                                                                 #       INTERSECTION WITH THE STRAIGHT LINE SECTION OF THE SWITCH CURVE...
041099,000123:                                                                                                 #       THE INTERSECTION DEFINES DESIRED OGARATE. IF THE OPERATING POINT IS
041100,000124:                                                                                                 #       CLOSE TO THE SWITCH LINE, THE APPROXIMATION IS QUITE GOOD (INDEED
041101,000125:                                                                                                 #       THE APPROXIMATE AND QUADRATIC SOLUTIONS CONVERGE IN THE LIMIT AS
041102,000126:                                                                                                 #       THE SWITCH LINE IS APPROACHED). IF THE OPERATING POINT IS NOT CLOSE
041103,000127:                                                                                                 #       TO THE SWITCH LINE, THE APPROXIMATE SOLUTION GIVES VALID TREND
041104,000128:                                                                                                 #       INFORMATION (DIRECTION OF DESIRED OGARATE) AT LEAST. THE 
041105,000129:                                                                                                 #       RE-EVALUATION OF DESIRED OGARATE IN SUBSEQUENT ROLL DAP PASSES (1/2
041106,000130:                                                                                                 #       SECOND INTERVALS) WILL BENEFIT FROM THE CONVERGENT NATURE OF THE
041107,000131:                                                                                                 #       APPROXIMATION.
041108,000132: 
041109,000133:                                                                                                 #  FOR LARGE OGAERROR THE TANGENT INTERSECTS +-MINLIM SWITCH BOUNDRY BEFORE
041110,000134:                                                                                                 #       INTERSECTING THE STRAIGHT LINE SWITCH. HOWEVER THE MINLIM IS
041111,000135:                                                                                                 #       IGNORED IN COMPUTING THE FIRING TIME, SO THAT THE EXTENSION (INTO
041112,000136:                                                                                                 #       THE COAST REGION) OF THE STRAIGHT LINE SWITCH IS WHAT IS FIRED TO.
041113,000137:                                                                                                 #       IF THE ROLL DAP FINDS ITSELF IN THE COAST REGION BEFORE REACHING
041114,000138:                                                                                                 #       THE DESIRED INTERSECTION (IE, IN THE REGION BETWEEN THE MINLIM
041115,000139:                                                                                                 #       AND THE STRAIGHT LINE SWITCH) IT WILL EXIBIT NORMAL COAST-REGION
041116,000140:                                                                                                 #       BEHAVIOR AND TURN OFF THE JETS. THE PURPOSE OF THIS FIRING POLICY
041117,000141:                                                                                                 #       IS TO MAINTAIN STATIC ROLL STABILITY IN THE EVENT OF A JET
041118,000142:                                                                                                 #       FAILED-ON.
041119,000143: 
041120,000144:                                                                                                 #  WHEN THE OPERATING POINT IS IN REGION 1 THE SAME APPROXIMATION IS
041121,000145:                                                                                                 #       MADE, BUT AT AN ARTIFICIALLY-CREATED OR DUMMY OPERATING POINT,
041122,000146:                                                                                                 #       DEFINED BY.. OGAERROR = INTERSECTION OF CONTROL PARABOLA AND
041123,000147:                                                                                                 #       OGAERROR AXIS, OGARATE = +-LMCRATE WHERE SIGN IS OPPOSITE THAT OF
041124,000148:                                                                                                 #       REAL OPERATING POINT RATE.  WHEN THE OPERATING POINT HAS PASSED
041125,000149:                                                                                                 #       FROM REGION 1 TO REGION 6, THE DUMMY POINT IS NO LONGER REQUIRED,
041126,000150:                                                                                                 #       AND THE SOLUTION REVERTS TO THAT OF A REGULAR REGION 6 POINT.
041127,000151:

041130,000154:                                                                                                 #                                  2
041131,000155:                                                                                                 #      SOGAERROR = (DB - (SOGARATE) (1/CONACC)/2) SGN(SOGARATE)

041135,000159:

041137,000161:

041139,000163:

041144,000168:                                                                                                 #                                  2

041147,000171:                                                                                                 #                   +LMCRATE
