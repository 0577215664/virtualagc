A team of volunteers performed the transcription and proof-reading. The scanned page images are available at the Virtual AGC Project website, as well as higher-quality (but much larger) images at the Virtual AGC Project's collection in the Internet Archive. Report any problems by creating "issues" at the Virtual AGC Project's GitHub Repository.

025305,000028:                                                                                                  #  THIS "WAITLIST TASK" IS EXECUTED EVERY 2 SECONDS DURING ASCENT (APS) BURNS.  IT ESTIMATES THE ACCELERATION DUE
025306,000029:                                                                                                  #  TO THE ENGINE OFF-SET.  TO USE THESE ESTIMATES, IT ALSO CALCULATES TERMS TO ADD INTO THE LM DAP RATE DERIVATION
025307,000030:                                                                                                  #  AND WEIGHTING FACTORS FOR THE ESTIMATE OF AOS AND FOR THE RATE DERIVATION.  FINALLY, IT ALSO SETS UP "AOSJOB"
025308,000031:                                                                                                  #  THROUGH THE EXECUTIVE TO CALCULATE FUMCTIONS OF THE AOS.
