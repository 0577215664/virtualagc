These source-code files were derived originally from the scan from MIT's Dibner Institute's now-discontinued website titled "History of Recent Science and Technology", which in turn was originally created by Gary Neff, though his high-quality scans were unfortunately reduced in legibility when presented online. The markings on the front of the printout imply that it was the original AGC developer Norm Brodeur's copy. A scan by Ron Burkey has superceded it, made from a more-legible copy from the collection of original AGC developer Fred Martin. The source code was transcribed from those images by Ron Burkey. Note that the page images presented online are of reduced quality, and that higher-quality images are available. Report any conversion errors or legibility problems in page images to info@sandroid.org. Notations on the program listing read, in part:

	ASSEMBLE REVISION 249 OF AGC PROGRAM COLOSSUS BY NASA 2021111-041

019744,000045:                                                                                                  #  PURPOSE
019745,000046:                                                                                                  #        (1)     TO CALCULATE THE REQUIRED DELTA V AND OTHER INITIAL CONDITIONS

019748,000049:                                                                                                  #                (A)     TIME OF IGNITION TIG (TPI) OR THE ELEVATION ANGLE (E) OF
019749,000050:                                                                                                  #                        THE ACTIVE/PASSIVE VEHICLE LOS AT TIG (TPI).
019750,000051:                                                                                                  #                (B)     CENTRAL ANGLE OF TRANSFER (CENTANG) FROM TIG (TPI) TO
019751,000052:                                                                                                  #                        INTERCEPT TIME (TIG (TPF)).
019752,000053:                                                                                                  #        (2)     TO CALCULATE TIG (TPI) GIVEN E OR E GIVEN TIG (TPI).
019753,000054:                                                                                                  #        (3)     TO CALCULATE THESE PARAMETERS BASED UPON MANEUVER DATA
019754,000055:                                                                                                  #                APPROVED AND KEYED INTO THE DSKY BY THE ASTRONAUT.
019755,000056:                                                                                                  #        (4)     TO DISPLAY TO THE ASTRONAUT AND THE GROUND CERTAIN DEPENDENT
019756,000057:                                                                                                  #                VARIABLES ASSOCIATED WITH THE MANEUVER FOR APPROVAL BY THE
019757,000058:                                                                                                  #                ASTRONAUT/GROUND.
019758,000059:                                                                                                  #        (5)     TO STORE THE TPI TARGET PARAMETERS FOR USE BY THE DESIRED
019759,000060:                                                                                                  #                THRUSTING PROGRAM.
019760,000061: 
019761,000062:                                                                                                  #  ASSUMPTIONS
019762,000063:                                                                                                  #        (1)     LM ONLY -- THIS PROGRAM IS BASED UPON PREVIOUS COMPLETION OF
019763,000064:                                                                                                  #                THE CONSTANT DELTA ALTITUDE (CDH) PROGRAM (P33/P73).
019764,000065:                                                                                                  #                THEREFORE --
019765,000066:                                                                                                  #                (A)     AT A SELECTED TPI TIME (NOW IN STORAGE) THE LINE OF SIGHT
019766,000067:                                                                                                  #                        BETWEEN THE ACTIVE AND PASSIVE VEHICLES WAS SELECTED TO BE
019767,000068:                                                                                                  #                        A PRESCRIBED ANGLE (E) (NOW IN STORAGE) FROM THE
019768,000069:                                                                                                  #                        HORIZONTAL PLANE DEFINED BY THE ACTIVE VEHICLE POSITION.
019769,000070:                                                                                                  #                (B)     THE TIME BETWEEN CDH IGNITION AND TPI IGNITION WAS
019770,000071:                                                                                                  #                        COMPUTED TO BE GREATER THAN 10 MINUTES.
019771,000072:                                                                                                  #                (C)     THE VARIATION OF TEH ALTITUDE DIFFERENCE BETWEEN THE
019772,000073:                                                                                                  #                        ORBITS WAS MINIMIZED.
019773,000074:                                                                                                  #                (D)     THE PERICENTER ALTITUDES OF ORBITS FOLLOWING CSI AND
019774,000075:                                                                                                  #                        CDH WERE COMPUTED TO BE GREATER THAN 35,000 FT FOR LUNAR

019776,000077:                                                                                                  #                        ORBIT OR 85 NM FOR EARTH ORBIT.
019777,000078:                                                                                                  #                (E)     THE CSI AND CDH MANEUVERS WERE ASSUMED TO BE PARALLEL TO
019778,000079:                                                                                                  #                        THE PLANE OF THE PASSIVE VEHICLE ORBIT.  HOWEVER, CREW
019779,000080:                                                                                                  #                        MODIFICATION OF DELTA V (LV) COMPONENTS MAY HAVE RESULTED
019780,000081:                                                                                                  #                        IN AN OUT-OF-PLANE MANEUVER.
019781,000082:                                                                                                  #        (2)     STATE VECTOR UPDATED BY P27 ARE DISALLOWED DURING AUTOMATIC
019782,000083:                                                                                                  #                STATE VECTOR UPDATING INITIATED BY P20 (SEE ASSUMPTION (4)).

019784,000085:                                                                                                  #                TIME GROUND PARTICIPATION IN DATA INPUT AND OUTPUT.  COMPUTED
019785,000086:                                                                                                  #                VARIABLES MAY BE STORED FOR LATER VERIFICATION BY THE GROUND.

019788,000089:                                                                                                  #                FLIGHT PLAN MANEUVER SEQUENCES.
019789,000090:                                                                                                  #        (4)     THE RENDEZVOUS RADAR MAY OR MAY NOT BE USED TO UPDATE THE LM
019790,000091:                                                                                                  #                OR CSM STATE VECTORS FOR THIS PROGRAM.  IF RADAR USE IS
019791,000092:                                                                                                  #                DESIRED THE RADAR WAS TURNED ON AND LOCKED ON THE CSM BY
019792,000093:                                                                                                  #                PREVIOUS SELECTION OF P20.  RADAR SIGHTING MARKS WILL BE MADE
019793,000094:                                                                                                  #                AUTOMATICALLY APPROXIMATELY ONCE A MINUTE WHEN ENABLED BY THE
019794,000095:                                                                                                  #                TRACK AND UPDATE FLAGS (SEE P20).  THE RENDEZVOUS TRACKING
019795,000096:                                                                                                  #                MARK COUNTER IS ZEROED BY TEH SELECTION OF P20 AND AFTER EACH
019796,000097:                                                                                                  #                THRUSTING MANEUVER.
019797,000098:                                                                                                  #        (5)     THE ISS NEED NOT BE ON TO COMPLETE THIS PROGRAM.
019798,000099:                                                                                                  #        (6)     THE OPERATION OF THE PROGRAM UTILIZES THE FOLLOWING FLAGS --

019801,000102:                                                                                                  #                        DOING RENDEZVOUS THRUSTING MANEUVERS TO THE PROGRAM WHICH

019803,000104:                                                                                                  #                        EACH RENDEZVOUS PRE-THRUSTING PROGRAM.

019806,000107:                                                                                                  #                        SELECTED THE FINAL MANEUVER COMPUTATION CYCLE.

019809,000110:                                                                                                  #                        REQUIRED FOR EXECUTION OF THIS MANEUVER BY THE THRUSTING
019810,000111:                                                                                                  #                        PROGRAM SELECTED AFTER COMPLETION OF THIS PROGRAM.

019812,000113:                                                                                                  #        (7)     ONCE THE PARAMETERS REQUIRED FOR COMPUTATION OF THE MANEUVER
019813,000114:                                                                                                  #                HAVE BEEN COMPLETELY SPECIFIED, TEH VALUE OF THE ACTIVE
019814,000115:                                                                                                  #                VEHICLE CENTRAL ANGLE OF TRANSFER IS COMPUTED AND STURED.

019819,000120:                                                                                                  #                CENTRAL ANGLE OF TRANSFER OF THE ACTIVE VEHICLE IS NOT WITHIN
