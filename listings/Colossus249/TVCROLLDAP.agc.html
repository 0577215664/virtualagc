Source Code

These source-code files were derived originally from the scan from MIT's Dibner Institute's now-discontinued website titled "History of Recent Science and Technology", which in turn was originally created by Gary Neff, though his high-quality scans were unfortunately reduced in legibility when presented online. The markings on the front of the printout imply that it was the original AGC developer Norm Brodeur's copy. A scan by Ron Burkey has superceded it, made from a more-legible copy from the collection of original AGC developer Fred Martin. The source code was transcribed from those images by Ron Burkey. Note that the page images presented online are of reduced quality, and that higher-quality images are available. Report any conversion errors or legibility problems in page images to info@sandroid.org. Notations on the program listing read, in part:

	ASSEMBLE REVISION 249 OF AGC PROGRAM COLOSSUS BY NASA 2021111-041
	20'35 OCT. 28,1968
Note that the date is the date of the printout, not the date of the program revision.

042094,000145:                                                                                                  #        CONTROL ACCELERATION CONACC, OR ITS RECIPROCAL, 1/CONACC, THE TVC

042096,000147:                                                                                                  #        FIRING TIME IS SIMPLY THAT REQUIRED TO ACHIEVE THE DESIRED OGARATE,
042097,000148:                                                                                                  #        SUBJECT TO TEH LIMITATIONS DISCUSSED UNDER FUNCTIONAL DESCRIPTION,
042098,000149:                                                                                                  #        ABOVE.
042099,000150: 
042100,000151:                                                                                                  #  THE THREE CONTROL REGIONS  (+, -, AND ZERO TORQUE) ARE COMPRISED OF
042101,000152:                                                                                                  #        TWELVE SUBSET REGIONS  ( 1...6, AND THE CORRESPONDING 1-PRIME...
042102,000153:                                                                                                  #        5-PRIME )  SEE SECTION 3 OF THE GSOP  (SUNDISK OR COLOSSUS)
042103,000154:                                                                                                  #  Page 954
042104,000155: 
042105,000156:                                                                                                  #  GIVEN THE OPERATING POINT NOT IN THE COAST REGION, THE DESIRED OGARATE
042106,000157:                                                                                                  #        IS AT THE POINT OF PENETRATION OF THE COAST REGION BY THE CONTROL
042107,000158:                                                                                                  #        PARABOLA WHICH PASSES THROUGH THE OPERATING POINT.  FOR REGION 3
042108,000159:                                                                                                  #        DESIRED OGARATE IS SIMPLY +-MAXLIM.  FOR REGIONS 1 OR 6 THE SOLUTION
042109,000160:                                                                                                  #        TO A QUADRATIC IS REQUIRED (THE PENETRATION IS ALONG THE STRAIGHT
042110,000161:                                                                                                  #        LINE OR MINLIM BOUNDRY SWITCH LINES).  AN APPROXIMATION IS MADE
042111,000162:                                                                                                  #        INSTEAD.  CONSIDER AN OPERATING POINT IN REGION 6.  PASS A TANGENT TO
042112,000163:                                                                                                  #        THE CONTROL PARABOLA THROUGH THE OPERATING POINT, AND FIND ITS
042113,000164:                                                                                                  #        INTERSECTION WITH THE STRAIGHT LINE SECTION OF THE SWITCH CURVE...
042114,000165:                                                                                                  #        THE INTERSECTION DEFINES THE DESIRED OGARATE.  IF THE OPERATING POINT IS
042115,000166:                                                                                                  #        CLOSE TO THE SWITCH LINE, THE APPROXIMATION IS QUITE GOOD (INDEED
042116,000167:                                                                                                  #        THE APPROXIMATE AND QUADRATIC SOLUTIONS CONVERGE IN THE LIMIT AS
042117,000168:                                                                                                  #        THE SWITCH LINE IS APPROACHED).  IF THE OPERATING POINT IS NOT CLOSE
042118,000169:                                                                                                  #        TO THE SWITCH LINE, THE APPROXIMATE SOLUTION GIVES VALID TREND
042119,000170:                                                                                                  #        INFORMATION (DIRECTION OF DESIRED OGARATE) AT LEAST.  THE 
042120,000171:                                                                                                  #        RE-EVALUATION OF DESIRED OGARATE IN SUBSEQUENT ROLL DAP PASSES (1/2
042121,000172:                                                                                                  #        SECOND INTERVALS) WILL BENEFIT FROM THE CONVERGENT NATURE OF THE
042122,000173:                                                                                                  #        APPROXIMATION.
042123,000174: 
042124,000175:                                                                                                  #  FOR LARGE OGAERROR THE TANGENT INTERSECTS +-MINLIM SWITCH BOUNDRY BEFORE
042125,000176:                                                                                                  #        INTERSECTING THE STRAIGHT LINE SWITCH.  HOWEVER THE MINLIM IS
042126,000177:                                                                                                  #        IGNORED IN COMPUTING THE FIRING TIME, SO THAT THE EXTENSION (INTO
042127,000178:                                                                                                  #        THE COAST REGION) OF THE STRAIGHT LINE SWITCH IS WHAT IS FIRED TO.
042128,000179:                                                                                                  #        IF THE ROLL DAP FINDS ITSELF IN THE COAST REGION BEFORE REACHING
042129,000180:                                                                                                  #        THE DESIRED INTERSECTION (I.E., IN THE REGION BETWEEN THE MINLIM
042130,000181:                                                                                                  #        AND THE STRAIGHT LINE SWITCH) IT WILL EXHIBIT NORMAL COAST-REGION
042131,000182:                                                                                                  #        BEHAVIOR AND TURN OFF THE JETS.  THE PURPOSE OF THIS FIRING POLICY
042132,000183:                                                                                                  #        IS TO MAINTAIN STATIC ROLL STABILITY IN THE EVENT OF A JET
042133,000184:                                                                                                  #        FAILED-ON.
042134,000185: 
042135,000186:                                                                                                  #  WHEN THE OPERATING POINT IS IN REGION 1 THE SAME APPROXIMATION IS
042136,000187:                                                                                                  #        MADE, BUT AT AN ARTIFICIALLY-CREATED OR DUMMY OPERATING POINT,
042137,000188:                                                                                                  #        DEFINED BY:  OGAERROR = INTERSECTION OF CONTROL PARABOLA AND
042138,000189:                                                                                                  #        OGAERROR AXIS, OGARATE = +-LMCRATE WHERE SIGN IS OPPOSITE THAT OF
042139,000190:                                                                                                  #        REAL OPERATING POINT RATE.  WHEN THE OPERATING POINT HAS PASSED
042140,000191:                                                                                                  #        FROM REGION 1 TO REGION 6, THE DUMMY POINT IS NO LONGER REQUIRED,
042141,000192:                                                                                                  #        AND THE SOLUTION REVERTS TO THAT OF A REGULAR REGION 6 POINT.
