033977,000130:                                                                                                 #  disconnected from their respective variables

033980,000133:                                                                                                 #  THERE IS NO DAMPING FACTOR.  THE CONSTANTS KIGNX/B4, KIGNY/B8 AND KIGNV/B4 ARE ALL NEGATIVE IN SIGN.
