064127,000040:                                                                                                  #  PROGRAM DESCRIPTION:
064128,000041:                                                                                                  #        1/ACCS PROVIDES THE INTERFACE BETWEEN THE GUIDANCE PROGRAMS AND THE DIGITAL AUTOPILOT.  WHENEVER THERE IS A
064129,000042:                                                                                                  #        CHANGE IN THE MASS OF THE VEHICLE, IN THE DEADBAND SELECTED, IN THE VEHICLE CONFIGURATION (ASCENT-DESCENT-
064130,000043:                                                                                                  #        DOCKED), AND DURING A FRESH START OR A RESTART, 1/ACCS IS CALLED TO COMMUNICATE THE DATA CHANGES TO THE DAP.
064131,000044: 
064132,000045:                                                                                                  #        THE INPUTS TO 1/ACCS ARE MASS, ACCELERATION (ABDELV), DEADBAND (DB), OFFSET ACCELERATIONS (AOSQ AND AOSR),
064133,000046:                                                                                                  #        STAGE VERIFY BIT (CHAN30, BIT2), DOCKED BIT (DAPBOOLS, BIT13), DRIFT BIT (DAPBOOLS, BIT8), USEQRJTS (DAPBOOLS,
064134,000047:                                                                                                  #        BIT14), AND SURFACE FLAG (FLAGWRDB, BIT8), AND CH5MASK.
064135,000048: 
064136,000049:                                                                                                  #        1/ACCS COMPUTES THE JET ACCELERATIONS (1JACC, 1JACCQ, 1JACCR) AS FUNCTIONS OF MASS.  1JACCU AND 1JACCV ARE
064137,000050:                                                                                                  #        FORMED BY RESOLVING 1JACCQ NAD 1JACCR.  IN THE DESCENT CASE, THE DESCENT ENGINE MOMENT ARM (L, PVT-CG) IS ALSO
064138,000051:                                                                                                  #        COMPUTED AS A FUNCTION OF MASS.  THE RATE OF CHANGE OF ACCELERATION DUE TO ROTATION OF THE GIMBAL (ACCDOTQ,
064139,000052:                                                                                                  #        ACCDOTR) IS ALSO COMPUTED IN THE DESCENT CASE.
064140,000053: 
064141,000054:                                                                                                  #        AFTER THE ABOVE COMPUTATIONS, THE PROGRAM 1/ACCONT COMPUTES THE RECIPROCAL NET ACCELERATIONS ABOUT THE P, U,
064142,000055:                                                                                                  #        AND V AXES (2 JETS FOR P AXIS, BOTH 1 AND 2 JETS FOR U AND V AXES), AND THE RECIPROCAL COAST ACCELERATIONS ABOUT
064143,000056:                                                                                                  #        THE P, U, AND V AXES.  THE ACCELERATION FUNCTIONS (ACCFCTZ1 AND ACCFCTZ5) ARE ALSO COMPUTED FOR THESE AXES.  THE
064144,000057:                                                                                                  #        FIRE AND COAST DEADBANDS AND AXISDIST ARE COMPUTED FOR EACH AXIS.  FLAT AND ZONE3LIM, THE WIDTH AND HEIGHT OF THE
064145,000058:                                                                                                  #        MINIMUM IMPULSE ZONE, ARE COMPUTED.  1/ACCONT ALSO SETS ACCSWU AND ACCSWV, WHICH INDICATE WHEN 1 JET ACCELERATION
064146,000059:                                                                                                  #        IS NOT SUFFICIENT TO PRODUCE MINIMUM ACCELERATION.  AT THE COMPLETION OF 1/ACCS, THE ACCSOKAY BIT IS SET.
