063843,000038:                                                                                                  #  PROGRAM DESCRIPTION:
063844,000039:                                                                                                  #        1/ACCS PROVIDES THE INTERFACE BETWEEN THE GUIDANCE PROGRAMS AND THE DIGITAL AUTOPILOT.  WHENEVER THERE IS A
063845,000040:                                                                                                  #        CHANGE IN THE MASS OF THE VEHICLE, IN THE DEADBAND SELECTED, IN THE VEHICLE CONFIGURATION (ASCENT-DESCENT-
063846,000041:                                                                                                  #        DOCKED), AND DURING A FRESH START OR A RESTART, 1/ACCS IS CALLED TO COMMUNICATE THE DATA CHANGES TO THE DAP.
063847,000042: 
063848,000043:                                                                                                  #        THE INPUTS TO 1/ACCS ARE MASS, ACCELERATION (ABDELV), DEADBAND (DB), OFFSET ACCELERATIONS (AOSQ AND AOSR),
063849,000044:                                                                                                  #        STAGE VERIFY BIT (CHAN30, BIT2), DOCKED BIT (DAPBOOLS, BIT13), DRIFT BIT (DAPBOOLS, BIT8), USEQRJTS (DAPBOOLS,
063850,000045:                                                                                                  #        BIT14), AND SURFACE FLAG (FLAGWRDB, BIT8), AND CH5MASK.
063851,000046: 
063852,000047:                                                                                                  #        1/ACCS COMPUTES THE JET ACCELERATIONS (1JACC, 1JACCQ, 1JACCR) AS FUNCTIONS OF MASS.  1JACCU AND 1JACCV ARE
063853,000048:                                                                                                  #        FORMED BY RESOLVING 1JACCQ AND 1JACCR.  IN THE DESCENT CASE, THE DESCENT ENGINE MOMENT ARM (L, PVT-CG) IS ALSO
063854,000049:                                                                                                  #        COMPUTED AS A FUNCTION OF MASS.  THE RATE OF CHANGE OF ACCELERATION DUE TO ROTATION OF THE GIMBAL (ACCDOTQ,
063855,000050:                                                                                                  #        ACCDOTR) IS ALSO COMPUTED IN THE DESCENT CASE.
063856,000051: 
063857,000052:                                                                                                  #        AFTER THE ABOVE COMPUTATIONS, THE PROGRAM 1/ACCONT COMPUTES THE RECIPROCAL NET ACCELERATIONS ABOUT THE P, U,
063858,000053:                                                                                                  #        AND V AXES (2 JETS FOR P-AXIS, BOTH 1 AND 2 JETS FOR U AND V AXES), AND THE RECIPROCAL COAST ACCELERATIONS ABOUT
063859,000054:                                                                                                  #        THE P, U, AND V AXES.  THE ACCELERATION FUNCTIONS (ACCFCTZ1 AND ACCFCTZ5) ARE ALSO COMPUTED FOR THESE AXES.  THE
063860,000055:                                                                                                  #        FIRE AND COAST DEADBANDS AND AXISDIST ARE COMPUTED FOR EACH AXIS.  FLAT AND ZONE3LIM, THE WIDTH AND HEIGHT OF THE
063861,000056:                                                                                                  #        MINIMUM IMPULSE ZONE, ARE COMPUTED.  1/ACCONT ALSO SETS ACCSWU AND ACCSWV, WHICH INDICATE WHEN 1 JET ACCELERATION
063862,000057:                                                                                                  #        IS NOT SUFFICIENT TO PRODUCE MINIMUM ACCELERATION.  AT THE COMPLETION OF 1/ACCS, THE ACCSOKAY BIT IS SET.
063863,000058:
