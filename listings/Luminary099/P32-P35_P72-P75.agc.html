026478,000035:                                                                                                 #  PURPOSE
026479,000036: 
026480,000037:                                                                                                 #         (1)     TO CALCULATE PARAMETERS ASSOCIATED WTIH THE TIME FOLLOWING
026481,000038:                                                                                                 #                 CONCENTRIC FLIGHT PLAN MANEUVERS -- THE CO-ELLIPTIC SEQUENCE
026482,000039:                                                                                                 #                 INITIATION (CSI) MANEUVER AND THE CONSTANT DELTA ALTITUDE
026483,000040:                                                                                                 #                 (CDH) MANEUVER.
026484,000041: 
026485,000042:                                                                                                 #         (2)     TO CALCULATE THESE PARAMETERS BASED UPON MANEUVER DATA
026486,000043:                                                                                                 #                 APPROVED AND KEYED INTO THE DSKY BY THE ASTRONAUT.
026487,000044: 
026488,000045:                                                                                                 #         (3)     TO DISPLAY TO THE ASTRONAUT AND THE GROUND DEPENDENT VARIABLES
026489,000046:                                                                                                 #                 ASSOCIATED WITH THE CONCENTRIC FLIGHT PLAN MANEUVERS FOR
026490,000047:                                                                                                 #                 APPROVAL BY THE ASTRRONAUT/GROUND.
026491,000048: 
026492,000049:                                                                                                 #         (4)     TO STORE THE CSI TARGET PARAMETERS FOR USE BY THE DESIRED
026493,000050:                                                                                                 #                 THRUSTING PROGRAM.
026494,000051: 
026495,000052:                                                                                                 #  ASSUMPTIONS
026496,000053: 
026497,000054:                                                                                                 #         (1)     AT A SELECTED TPI TIME THE LINE OF SIGNT BETWEEN THE ACTIVE
026498,000055:                                                                                                 #                 AND PASSIVE VEHICLES IS SELECTED TO BE A PRESCRIBED ANGLE (E)
026499,000056:                                                                                                 #                 FROM THE HORIZONTAL PLANE DEFINED BY THE ACTIVE VEHICLE
026500,000057:                                                                                                 #                 POSITION.
026501,000058: 
026502,000059:                                                                                                 #         (2)     THE TIME BETWEEN CSI IGNITION AND CDH IGNITION MUST BE
026503,000060:                                                                                                 #                 COMPUTED TO BE GREATER THAN 10 MINUTES FOR SUCCESSFUL
026504,000061:                                                                                                 #                 COMPLETION OF THE PROGRAM.
026505,000062: 
026506,000063:                                                                                                 #         (3)     THE TIME BETWEEN CDH IGNITION AND TPI IGNITION MUST BE
026507,000064:                                                                                                 #                 COMPUTED TO BE GREATER THAN 10 MINUTES FOR SUCCESSFUL
026508,000065:                                                                                                 #                 COMPLETION OF THE PROGRAM.
026509,000066: 
026510,000067:                                                                                                 #         (4)     CDH DELTA V IS SELECTED TO MINIMIZE THE VARIATION OF THE
026511,000068:                                                                                                 #                 ALTITUDE DIFFERENCE BETWEEN THE ORBITS.
026512,000069: 
026513,000070:                                                                                                 #         (5)     CSI BURN IS DEFINED SUCH THAT THE IMPULSIVE DELTA V IS IN THE
026514,000071:                                                                                                 #                 HORIZONTAL PLANE DEFINED BY THE ACTIVE VEHICLE POSITION AT CSI
026515,000072:                                                                                                 #                 IGNITION.
026516,000073: 
026517,000074:                                                                                                 #         (6)     THE PERICENTER ALTITUDE OF THE ORBIT FOLLOWING CSI AND CDH
026518,000075:                                                                                                 #                 MUST BE GREATER THAN 35,000 FT (LUNAR ORBIT) OR 85 NM (EARCH
026519,000076:                                                                                                 #                 ORBIT) FOR SUCCESSFUL COMPLETION OF THIS PROGRAM.
026520,000077: 
026521,000078:                                                                                                 #         (7)     THE CSI AND CDH MANEUVERS ARE ORIGINALLY ASSUMED TO BE
026522,000079:                                                                                                 #                 PARALLEL TO THE PLANE OF THE CSM ORBIT.  HOWEVER, CREW
026523,000080:                                                                                                 #  Page 619
026524,000081:                                                                                                 #                 MODIFICATION OF DELTA V (LV) COMPONENTS MAY RESULT IN AN
026525,000082:                                                                                                 #                 OUT-OF-PLANE CSI MANEUVER
026526,000083: 
026527,000084:                                                                                                 #         (8)     STATE VECTOR UPDATES BY P27 ARE DISALLOWED DURING AUTOMATIC
026528,000085:                                                                                                 #                 STATE VECTOR UPDATING INITIATED BY P20 (SEE ASSUMPTION 10).
026529,000086: 
026530,000087:                                                                                                 #         (9)     COMPUTED VARIABLES MAY BE STORED FOR LATER VERIFICATION BY
026531,000088:                                                                                                 #                 THE GROUND.  THESE STORAGE CAPABILITIES ARE NORMALLY LIMITED
026532,000089:                                                                                                 #                 ONLY TO THE PARAMETERS FOR ONE THRUSTING MANEUVER AT A TIME
026533,000090:                                                                                                 #                 EXCEPT FOR CONCENTRIC FLIGHT PLAN MANEUVER SEQUENCES.
026534,000091: 
026535,000092:                                                                                                 #         (10)    THE RENDEZVOUS RADAR MAY OR MAY NOT BE USED TO UPDATE THE LM
026536,000093:                                                                                                 #                 OR CSM STATE VECTORS FOR THIS PROGRAM.  IF RADAR USE IS
026537,000094:                                                                                                 #                 DESIRED THE RADAR WAS TURNED ON AND LOCKED BY THE CSM BY
026538,000095:                                                                                                 #                 PREVIOUS SELECTION OF P20.  RADAR SIGHTING MARKS WILL BE MADE
026539,000096:                                                                                                 #                 AUTOMATICALLY APPROXIMATELY ONCE A MINUTE WHEN ENABLED BY THE
026540,000097:                                                                                                 #                 TRACK AND UPDATE FLAGS (SEE P20).  THE RENDEZVOUS TRACKING
026541,000098:                                                                                                 #                 MARK COUNTER IS ZEROED BY TEH SELECTION OF P20 AND AFTER EACH
026542,000099:                                                                                                 #                 THRUSTING MANEUVER.
026543,000100: 
026544,000101:                                                                                                 #         (11)    THE ISS NEED NOT BE ON TO COMPLETE THIS PROGRAM.
026545,000102: 
026546,000103:                                                                                                 #         (12)    THE OPERATION OF THE PROGRAM UTILIZES THE FOLLOWING FLAGS --
026547,000104: 
026548,000105:                                                                                                 #                         ACTIVE VEHICLE FLAG -- DESIGNATES THE VEHICLE WHICH IS

026555,000112:                                                                                                 #                         CYCLE.

026558,000115:                                                                                                 #                         STEERING REQUIRED FOR EXECUTION OF THIS MANEUVER BY THE
026559,000116:                                                                                                 #                         THRUSTING PROGRAM SELECTED AFTER COMPLETION OF THIS
026560,000117:                                                                                                 #                         PROGRAM.
